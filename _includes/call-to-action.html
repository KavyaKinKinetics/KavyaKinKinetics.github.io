About KinKinetics

KinKinetics is a pioneering neuroadaptive company on a mission to support human resilience in the most challenging environments—space, healthcare, and beyond.

We develop intelligent, AI-driven systems that combine psychological insight, physiological monitoring, and real-time decision support to help individuals thrive in isolation, high-stakes settings, and emotionally demanding scenarios. Whether it’s astronauts on long-duration space missions, elderly individuals living alone, or remote healthcare teams, our tools are built to offer kin-like emotional support and cognitive clarity when it matters most.

Our work is grounded in cutting-edge neuroeconomics, behavioral science, space medicine and space psychology, and we collaborate with scientists, engineers, doctors and caregivers across the globe to build solutions that are adaptive, evidence-based, and deeply human.

From wearable technologies and stress-intervention protocols to clinical trials on oxytocin and vagus nerve stimulation, KinKinetics is creating the next generation of resilience tools—for Earth and space.
