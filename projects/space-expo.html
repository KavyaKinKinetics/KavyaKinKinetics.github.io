Space Expo 2025

Space Expo 2025 Excel London.

KinKinetics had the exceptional opportunity to participate in the dynamic Space-Comm Expo 2025 in London. The event was a true hub of innovation, where brilliant minds from across the space industry came together to share insights, explore future missions, and shape the roadmap of space exploration. Over two energizing days, KinKinetics engaged in powerful conversations about the future of human spaceflight and the vital role of emotional and psychological support in long-duration missions.

One of the most surreal and memorable experiences was meeting ESA astronaut Tim Peake CMG. His book, "Ask an Astronaut," was the very spark that ignited our founder’s passion for space—so to meet him in person felt like a dream realized. Listening to his thoughts on the space economy, future missions, and the evolving landscape of human spaceflight was not only inspiring, but also deeply affirming of KinKinetics’ mission to provide kin-like psychological support systems for space crews.

We were also incredibly fortunate to meet Professor Brian Cox, whose enthusiasm for science and exploration radiated through every word. His presence brought a unique blend of inspiration and clarity to the event, setting the perfect tone for meaningful dialogue. His encouragement and ideas reminded us of the beauty of curiosity—the very essence that drives innovation in space and beyond.

The conference was filled with insightful sessions, but one that stood out was “The Biology of the Final Frontier,” featuring Dr. Carina Kern, Prof. Damian Bailey, and Keith Siew. Their discussion emphasized the importance of addressing the biological and psychological challenges of long-range spaceflight—an area that KinKinetics is deeply invested in. It was a powerful validation of our ongoing work in creating support systems focused on human resilience and well-being in extreme environments.

From reconnecting with incredible professionals from previous accelerator programs to engaging in spontaneous conversations with new collaborators, Space-Comm Expo 2025 proved to be a place where ideas flourish and partnerships form. We extend our sincere thanks to the organizers and all those who made the event such a success.

The momentum doesn’t stop here—KinKinetics is thrilled to announce that we will be heading to Space-Comm Expo Dubai, where we will be exhibiting at our very own stall. This marks the next exciting chapter in our journey, and we can’t wait to showcase our solutions and connect with the global space community.
